## 1. Business Overview

Domani is an evening planning productivity app built around the concept "Plan Tomorrow Tonight, Wake Up Ready to Execute." The app helps users plan their next day each evening when they're calm and reflective, reducing morning decision fatigue and enabling focused execution from the moment they wake up.

**Product offering:**
- 14-day free trial (full access, no credit card required)
- Lifetime purchase: $34.99 general / $9.99 early adopter (one-time, no subscription)
- All future updates included

**Key differentiators:**
- Evening-first planning philosophy (no competitor owns this)
- Plan Lock feature (lock your plan to prevent overthinking)
- The 3-6 Rule (science-backed task limits)
- Calm, purposeful brand voice
- One-time lifetime pricing vs competitor subscriptions (Sunsama $20/mo, Motion $19/mo)

## 3. Target Audience

**Primary audience:** Broad productivity seekers — anyone wanting to be more organized and productive.

**Segments:**
- **Busy professionals** — Managers, consultants, executives overwhelmed by morning inbox and priorities
- **Knowledge workers** — Anyone doing cognitive work who needs focus and daily planning
- **Entrepreneurs & creators** — Founders, freelancers who struggle with scattered focus and procrastination
- **Students & self-improvers** — People building better habits and routines

**Common pain points:**
- Wake up stressed, scrambling to figure out priorities
- Morning decision fatigue wastes mental energy
- Reactive mode (emails/messages hijack the day)
- Lost momentum by mid-morning
- Too many tasks, no clear focus

**Search behavior:**
- Problem-aware searches: "morning decision fatigue," "overwhelmed every morning," "chaotic mornings"
- Solution-aware searches: "evening planning routine," "plan day night before," "daily planner app"
- Comparison searches: "sunsama alternative," "best daily planner app 2026"

## 5. Content Strategy

### Niche Ownership: "Evening Planning"
The biggest SEO opportunity is that **no major competitor owns "evening planning" as a category**. Domani should claim this niche aggressively through content.

### Content Pillars
1. **Evening Planning** (pillar) — The complete guide to evening planning, methodology, science
2. **Morning Productivity** — How evening planning transforms mornings
3. **Decision Fatigue** — Science of decision fatigue and how to combat it
4. **Productivity Methods** — Ivy Lee method, MIT framework, 3-6 Rule
5. **Comparisons** — Domani vs competitors, "best of" roundups

### Content Cadence
- 1-2 blog posts per week
- 1 comparison page per month
- Quarterly pillar content updates

### Brand Voice Constraint
All content must match Domani's calm, purposeful, empathetic tone. No aggressive marketing language. Focus on the feeling of waking up ready, not productivity guilt.
